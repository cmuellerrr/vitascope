Here's how it works

We help you tell the world about your business, and we have a pretty simple process for getting ideas out of your head (and ours) and onto the screen.

We plan every detail

After working with you to define the concept, we write a detailed script and storyboard with locations, characters, etc.

If you like what you see, we start locking down locations, actors, and equipment.

Shoot everything in sight

Once we build a shot list, we move into full production where we'll spend anywhere from 1 to 5 days filming.

We're perfectionists, so we take time to make sure we get everything we need.

Make it perfect

Then we get to start editing. This includes the fun stuff like color grading, sound mixing, and score composition.

And we believe in teamwork, so we'll work with you to get feedback on each draft.

Then deliver the goods

When we love it, and you love it, we'll apply the finishing touches and export the video to any formats you need so you can show it to the world and watch the customers roll in.

That's it!
